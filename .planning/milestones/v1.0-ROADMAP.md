## Milestone Summary

**Key Accomplishments:**
- Centralized metadata, canonical, robots, sitemap, and schema behavior behind shared contracts and checks.
- Converted feature/industry SEO surfaces to typed data contracts with deterministic linking and expansion guardrails.
- Shipped competitor comparison and authority content architecture for Connecteam and Workyard intent capture.
- Replaced simulated lead handling with real endpoint flow and standardized booked-call attribution taxonomy.
- Added deterministic booked-call and weekly SEO reporting exports segmented by cluster/template/landing URL.
- Added release-blocking CI workflows for SEO quality, AI publish-gate validation, and recurring reporting automation.
