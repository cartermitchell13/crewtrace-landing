## The Growing Problem with Manual Tracking

Summit HVAC technicians moved between multiple jobs in a single day, which made manual time capture especially error-prone. The office team spent hours each week reconciling overlaps, missing entries, and conflicting notes.

Payroll lock regularly slipped because disputes were identified too late and lacked clear proof.

## Implementing the Crewtrace Framework

Instead of forcing a full cutover, Summit started with a one-crew pilot and measured payroll-close outcomes weekly. This phased approach let the team fix process gaps before broad rollout.

Supervisors and office reviewers followed proof-led workflows so exception handling was consistent across the organization.

## Tangible Business Impact

Summit reduced payroll dispute tickets by 42% and cut weekly review time from five hours to under two. More importantly, they built trust in the process because every overtime decision was tied to verifiable field activity.

The migration succeeded because it balanced operational continuity with accountability.
